Emails I Can't Send is the fifth studio album by American singer and songwriter Sabrina Carpenter released on July 15, 2022, by Island Records. It is Carpenter's first album under the label. It is primarily a pop record with elements of folk-pop, dance-pop and storytelling, themed around emails and messages Carpenter wrote but did not send.
